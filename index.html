Process
How Sheng Shop works
1. Layout and sizing
Share room photos, plans and measurements so we can propose headboard width, sofa depth and seating heights.
2. Frame and comfort
We tune the frame, foam density and cushion profile to fit how you like to read, watch shows or host guests.
3. Fabric selection
Touch and see fabric in person. We mark out wall colors, flooring and carpentry tones when deciding.
4. Delivery and installation
Our team brings in the headboard, sofa or settee, protects your flooring and handles simple alignment on site.
